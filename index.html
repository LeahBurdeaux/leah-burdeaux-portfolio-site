Olive Health

Olive Health acts as a hub for the medical information for entire families, allowing patients to visualize, analyze, and share that information in any way they choose. Olive Health connects to multiple patient portals, popular fitness and diet tracking apps, and gives patients the ability to upload files to their accounts easily.

BrainFog

BrainFog was designed to help make learning large amounts of information fun. Through competitive and interactive trivia-like quizzes, BrainFog gamifies learning and helps anyone, from students learning early math skills, to professionals learning complex, high-stakes facts, memorize and recall the information they need.
